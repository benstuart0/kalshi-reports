The design effect (cluster SE / naive SE) shows how much correlation inflates uncertainty; values > 2 mean the effective sample size is < 25% of the raw fill count. If the worst-markout events are concentrated in a few correlated clusters, the session-wide markout may be driven by one or two bad outcomes rather than a systematic edge problem.
